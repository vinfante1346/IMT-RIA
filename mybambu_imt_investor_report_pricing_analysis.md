## Executive Summary

MyBambu's International Money Transfer (IMT) business processed **106,423 transactions** in October 2025, generating **$326,867 in revenue**. However, deep analysis reveals our current pricing is significantly below market standards, resulting in **cash pickup transactions losing money** and overall margins of only **19.9%**.

By implementing **market-competitive pricing**, we project revenue could increase to **$712,567** (+118%) with margins expanding to **63.3%**, adding **$4.6M in annual profit** without requiring additional transaction volume.

### Key Findings:

| Metric | Current Pricing | Proposed Pricing | Change |
|--------|----------------|------------------|--------|
| **Monthly Revenue** | $326,867 | $712,567 | +$385,700 (+118%) |
| **Monthly Margin** | $65,006 (19.9%) | $450,706 (63.3%) | +$385,700 (+593%) |
| **Annual Margin** | $780,077 | $5,408,472 | +$4,628,396 |
| **Customer LTV** | $9.11 | $63.19 | +$54.08 (+593%) |

## Proposed Pricing Strategy

### New Fee Structure (Market-Competitive Pricing)

| Fee Type | Current | Proposed | Change | % Increase |
|----------|---------|----------|--------|------------|
| **Credit (Bank) Fee** | $1.57 | **$2.99** | +$1.42 | +90.4% |
| **Cash Pickup Fee** | $2.49 | **$3.99** | +$1.50 | +60.2% |
| **FX Spread** | 0.73% | **2.00%** | +1.27 pts | +174% |

### Rationale for Each Change:

**Credit Fee: $1.57 ‚Üí $2.99**
- Industry standard: $1.99-$4.99
- Still cheaper than most competitors
- Aligns with customer willingness to pay for instant bank deposits

**Cash Fee: $2.49 ‚Üí $3.99**
- Industry standard: $3.99-$7.99
- **Necessary to achieve profitability** (currently losing $1.53 per txn)
- Still competitive with Remitly ($3.99-4.99)

**FX Spread: 0.73% ‚Üí 2.00%**
- Industry standard: 1.8-5.0%
- Still below most competitors
- Represents fair value for currency conversion service

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Customer Churn

**Risk Level:** Medium
**Probability:** 30-40% of one-time users may not return

**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Grandfathering:** Offer existing power users (10+ txns) 60-day grace period with old pricing
2. **Loyalty Program:** Launch VIP program for power users with discounted pricing
3. **First-Time User Promotion:** Keep promotional pricing for first 3 transactions to acquire users
4. **Value Communication:** Emphasize service improvements, speed, and reliability

**Expected Impact:** Limit churn increase to <5 percentage points (from 25.4% to <30.4%)

---

### Risk 2: Competitive Response

**Risk Level:** Low
**Probability:** 20% - competitors may reduce prices

**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Market Monitoring:** Track competitor pricing weekly
2. **Price Flexibility:** Be prepared to adjust FX spread if needed
3. **Value Differentiation:** Emphasize superior UX, speed, and customer service
4. **Corridor-Specific Pricing:** Maintain flexibility to price competitively in key corridors

**Expected Impact:** Our proposed pricing remains below most competitors; we have room to adjust

---

### Risk 3: Volume Decline

**Risk Level:** Medium
**Probability:** 15-25% potential volume decline in first 60 days

**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Increased Marketing:** Use higher CAC threshold ($18.96 vs $2.73) to drive acquisition
2. **Referral Program:** Launch aggressive referral incentives
3. **Promotional Campaigns:** Seasonal offers to drive volume
4. **Corridor Expansion:** Enter new high-margin corridors

**Expected Impact:** Volume may decline 10-15% initially but recover within 90 days with increased marketing
